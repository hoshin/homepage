About me

I'm a french developer. I've worked on various projects in domains ranging from pure development to auditing and on various subjects from healthcare to e-safes & API management platforms. When I get the opportunity, I also go back to my university to give a talk about "in real life" Agile methodologies.

Lately, I've mostly worked with Javascript (and NodeJS in particular).

When I'm not at work but still want to fiddle with code, I have a look at languages I don't quite know or new tools I want to try. Most of the work that is not just a more or less direct copy of a tutorial ends up on my github account. My most recent work as far as side projects go is a playoverwatch.com scrapper that aims to provide "distraction free stats" for Blizzard's Overwatch game

In the past, as a sort of follow-up to the app I realized for the Starcraft tournaments I hosted in an e-sports bar (Meltdown StarLeague), I built a tournament management app Toodle (source is available here : https://github.com/ToodleTM/toodle)
