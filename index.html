About me

I'm a french IT consultant. I've worked on various projects in domains ranging from pure development to auditing and on various subjects from healthcare to e-safes. When I get the opportunity, I also go back to my university to give a talk about "in real life" Agile methodologies.

Lately, my work gave me the opportunity to work on subjects more "ops oriented" (deployment, administration, performance, load testing ...), and on NodeJS.

On my spare time, as a sort of follow-up to the app I realized for the Starcraft tournaments I hosted in an e-sports bar (Meltdown StarLeague), I'm now coding tournament management app Toodle (source is available here : https://github.com/ToodleTM/toodle)
